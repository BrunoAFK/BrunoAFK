DevOps and platform engineer with a background in operations leadership. I build infrastructure that's reliable, cost-conscious, and useful: CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes, IaC, observability stacks. The kind of stuff that has to work in production.

I also carry years of experience on the business and operations side: project management, stakeholder communication, budgets, and delivery. That background means I can translate between technical decisions and business outcomes without losing something in translation.

Outside of client work, most of my energy goes into Home Assistant, Proxmox, and automation with n8n and AI. The projects here reflect that: practical tools built around real use cases, most of them AI-assisted.
